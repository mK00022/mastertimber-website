About Us

Master Timber is your one-stop woodworking supply store in Lydenburg, South Africa. We blend modern precision with traditional craftsmanship to bring you high-quality timber, plywood, MDF, melamine, and chipboard. Our expert cut & edge services ensure your materials are ready for use in any high-end custom furniture or cabinetry project.

Our Services

  • Custom Precision Cutting
  • Automated Edge Banding
  • Board Supply (MDF, Plywood, Melamine, Chipboard)
  • 3D Design and Furniture Component Manufacturing
